About

AI Audiovisual Automation professional in Film and Media based in Munich, Germany.

Bahre founded filmb in 2016 to bring worthwhile stories to life. Since then he has directed award-winning short films, brought cinema to children in Colombian favelas, co-founded Bus Film Lab in Bosnia, and built a body of work spanning film, theatre, documentary. He speaks six languages and currently creates at Allianz, Munich.

His documentary Ritual received great reviews after screening at City Kino Munich. Now finishing Ljubov and You Better Run Now, with Bosnian History Narrated — a 20-episode AI documentary series — in development.
